Welcome

Hello everyone and welcome to The Constellation! FrankThePegasus and her
community are some of the most welcoming people I've met on the
internet. No matter how long I'm away Frank and her community always
welcome me back like I was never gone.

Comfy Content

The thing I love the most about Franks content is that it is always
a relaxing space. You could show up in your pjs with a cup of tea and feel like
you've gone over to your girlfriends house on a lazy sunday. You can find her
playing things like...

  • Stardew Valley
  • My Time at Portia
  • Pokemon Let's Go

When I found her stream she was creating adorable characters out of perler beads.
She cares deeply about the people of The Constellation and can be found some days
just coloring and chatting with everyone about how their lives are going and
different mental health difficulties that plague us all. Sometimes you just need
a safe space and that space will always be with these people.
